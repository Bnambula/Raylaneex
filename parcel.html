Key Terms & Conditions
1. Liability: Raylane's maximum liability for loss or damage is limited to the declared value, or UGX 200,000, whichever is lower — unless UAP insurance was purchased.
2. Prohibited items: Live animals, flammable substances, illegal items, unmarked currency, and hazardous materials are strictly prohibited.
3. Collection: Recipient must present this tracking number and a valid ID (or NID if specified by sender) to collect. Parcels uncollected after 48 hours incur a UGX 2,000/day storage fee.
4. Claims: Any damage or loss claim must be reported within 24 hours of expected delivery. Claims with UAP insurance are processed within 5 business days.
5. Refunds: Parcel fees are non-refundable once the bus departs. Pre-dispatch cancellation: 80% refund if cancelled 2+ hours before bus departure.
6. Contents: Sender is responsible for accurate declaration. False declaration voids all insurance and may result in parcel confiscation.
