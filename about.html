Our Mission

Formalising Uganda's Transport Sector, One Booking at a Time

Uganda's road transport sector moves UGX 2.4 trillion per year. It is 92% cash-only, entirely analogue, and completely data-dark. Passengers have no reliable way to book verified seats. Operators have no system to manage their fleets, drivers, compliance, or finances.

Raylane Express changes that. We are building three things simultaneously: a passenger marketplace, an operator SaaS platform, and an internal fleet management system — each generating revenue independently, each making the others stronger.

🇺🇬

Uganda First

Every feature is designed around Ugandan realities — 2G networks, MoMo payments, right-hand-drive buses, and the Luganda-speaking passenger base.

📊

Data for Good

We turn the sector's data darkness into light — giving operators P&L clarity and giving planners (KCCA, NGOs) the route intelligence they've never had.

🔒

Safety Always

Verified operators, PSV compliance tracking, women's safety sections, and instant refunds — we treat safety as infrastructure, not a feature.
