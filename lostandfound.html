  • Items are held for 14 days at the relevant terminal.
  • After 14 days, unclaimed items are donated to charity.
  • Raylane Express is not liable for cash, jewellery, or undeclared valuables left on buses.
  • Our team checks with drivers within 6 hours of your report during business hours (7 AM–8 PM).
  • For urgent items (IDs, passports, medical records), call immediately: +256 700 000 001

Your Safety Is Our Priority

Every Raylane Express bus carries only verified, PSV-licenced drivers. All vehicles have valid insurance and roadworthiness certificates. Here's how to travel safely.

🎫 Verify Your Booking

  • Only board a bus that matches the operator name, route, and time on your QR ticket.
  • Your ticket has a unique QR code. Never share it with others.
  • If a conductor asks for additional payment beyond your ticket amount, refuse and report it.
  • All legitimate Raylane bookings are confirmed by SMS from our shortcode — not a random number.

👩 Women Travelling Alone

  • Request a window seat or front section when booking — this is supported in your preferences.
  • Our passenger welfare team (Mugala Maria) monitors women's safety reports. Report any incident via WhatsApp: +256 700 000 002
  • Save our emergency contact in your phone before travelling.
  • On selected routes, women-only seating sections are available — select this during booking.

🧳 Protecting Your Belongings

  • Keep valuables (phones, cash, documents) in your personal bag — never in overhead racks.
  • Do not leave items on the seat when you disembark for stopovers.
  • For high-value items, use our parcel system with UAP insurance rather than carrying in hand luggage.
  • Report suspicious behaviour to the driver or conductor immediately.

🚌 Bus & Driver Standards

  • All Raylane drivers hold valid PSV (Public Service Vehicle) licences.
  • Buses display a valid roadworthiness fitness certificate and insurance sticker.
  • If a bus appears unroadworthy or the driver appears impaired, do not board. Report to us immediately.
  • Speed limits and road safety regulations are enforced. Passengers may report reckless driving.
