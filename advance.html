Advance Booking Terms & Conditions

Please read and consent to the following terms before placing your advance booking deposit.

1. What is Advance Booking?

Advance booking allows you to reserve a seat on a future Raylane Express trip up to 30 days before the departure date. The price you see at the time of booking is locked — you will not be charged a higher fare if prices increase.

2. Deposit Requirement

  • A non-refundable commitment deposit of 20% of the total fare is required at the time of advance booking.
  • The remaining 80% balance must be paid no later than 24 hours before the scheduled departure time via MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, or at a Raylane terminal.
  • Failure to pay the balance by the 24-hour deadline will result in automatic cancellation of the booking and forfeiture of the 20% deposit.
  • A payment link for the balance will be sent to your registered phone number 48 hours before departure and again at 25 hours before departure.

3. Vehicle and Seat Allocation

  • Upon deposit payment, Raylane Express allocates a confirmed vehicle (coach or minivan as applicable) from its fleet or approved third-party operator fleet.
  • Seat preferences are recorded and honoured where possible but are not guaranteed. Specific seat numbers are assigned at the time of balance payment.
  • Raylane reserves the right to substitute a vehicle of equivalent or higher capacity without notice.

4. Refund Policy

The following refund schedule applies to advance bookings:

5. Passenger Responsibilities

  • Passengers must arrive at the designated boarding point at least 15 minutes before departure. Raylane is not obligated to hold the bus for late passengers.
  • The advance booking QR code / SMS reference must be presented at boarding. No boarding will be permitted without confirmation.
  • Passengers are responsible for confirming the exact boarding point (bus park, terminal, or designated stop) — this will be specified in your SMS confirmation.
  • One piece of luggage up to 20kg is included. Additional luggage or oversized items incur the standard parcel fee.

6. Changes and Amendments

  • Date changes: permitted once, free of charge, if requested more than 48 hours before original departure. Subject to availability on the new date.
  • Route changes: not permitted after deposit is paid. You must cancel and rebook.
  • Name changes: permitted up to 24 hours before departure — contact support via WhatsApp.

7. No-Show Policy

If a passenger does not board and has not cancelled, the booking is treated as a no-show. No refund of any amount (deposit or balance) is made for no-shows.

8. Force Majeure

Raylane Express is not liable for delays or cancellations caused by road closures, natural disasters, government action, civil unrest, or other events beyond its reasonable control. In such cases, passengers will be offered a free reschedule or full refund.
