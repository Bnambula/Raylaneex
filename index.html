Simple Process

Book in Under 60 Seconds

No account. No app download. Works on 2G. Just your phone number and a MoMo PIN.

1

Search Your Route

Enter origin, destination, and travel date. See all approved trips with real-time seat availability, operator ratings, and pricing.

2

Choose Your Seat

Select your exact seat from an interactive map of the bus. Your seat is locked for 5 minutes while you pay.

3

Pay via MoMo

Confirm with your MTN or Airtel MoMo PIN. Payment confirmed in ~8 seconds. QR e-ticket sent to your phone via SMS instantly.

4

Show Your QR Code

At boarding, show the QR code. Driver scans and confirms — green screen means you're cleared. Red screen shows the reason for any issue.

5

Earn Loyalty Points

Earn 1 point per UGX 100 spent. Accumulate to Silver and Gold for priority boarding, discounts, and free upgrades.

✓

No App Required

The entire flow works on WhatsApp, 2G SMS, or this website. We designed for Uganda's reality — your phone is all you need.

Trust & Safety

Built for Uganda's Reality

Every feature is designed around how Ugandans actually travel — with real-world constraints, not Silicon Valley assumptions.

🔒

Server-Locked Seats

Seat locks are enforced server-side with a 5-minute Redis timer. Zero overbooking. Your seat is secured the moment payment is confirmed.

📶

2G Compatible

The full booking flow — search, seat selection, MoMo payment, QR ticket — is optimised for 2G networks and low-end Android devices.

✅

Verified Operators Only

Every operator submits TIN, PSV licence, fleet insurance, and fitness certificates before being approved. Compliance is monitored continuously.

💸

Instant MoMo Refunds

Cancelled trips trigger automatic MoMo refunds to your original payment number — no forms, no waiting, no hassle.

👩

Women's Safety

Seat preferences include women-only sections where available. Every booking includes operator contact details and a safety report shortcode.

📦

Parcel OTP Delivery

Recipients confirm delivery with a unique OTP sent to their phone. Sender is notified immediately. Insurance claims processed within 5 business days.
